About Us

History

This 2024 the Project light it up was born, a foundation that aims to provide light in the roads that might emit dangers to the drivers along with the people in the community. We work to address the common problem that most communities face in our province. If this project continues to grow we will widen our project to help other communities in other provinces.

What we do?

As a student made project our aim is to alert the authorities about the dangers of roads that does not have lamp post or any light sources. We will try our best to achieve this goal in order to avoid further accidents, as some of us have observed it is more dangerous to be driving during nighttime specially if the roads are too dark.
